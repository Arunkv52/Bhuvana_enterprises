Product Description :

ASPEE – HTP horizontal triplex piston power spraying unit, developing 450 PSI (28 BAR) pressure and giving free discharge off 36 lpm at 950 rpm. Supplied with pulley, pressure vessel, pressure gauge, by pass cum pressure regular valve (CVA), 3 meter long bypass hose & suction hose with strainer, couplings for delivery hose.

  • Heavy duty & efficient sprayer
  • Triplex stainless steel piston and oil bath lubrication
  • Develops 400 psi pressure 
  • Discharge capacity is 36 lit per minute at 950 rpm 
  • Provided with coupling set 
  • Convenient to spray with 4 spray Gun at a time
  • Model: PS/16
  • Suction capacity: 36 ltr / min
  • Normal working pressure: 100 - 200 PSI (7 – 14 bar) 
  • Max. pressure: 400 PSI (28 bar)
  • Pump revolution: 950 rpm
  • Power required: 3 hp
  • Weight (without oil & pulley): 12 kg (approx.) 
  • L x W x H (in mm): 430 x 430 x 300
