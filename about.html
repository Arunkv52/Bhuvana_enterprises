Begin stage

Bhuvana Enterprises, based in Tamilnadu, focused on the Distribution, Sales, and Service of Agricultural Equipments and Farming Tools in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. The origin of Bhuvana Enterprises dates back to 1978, when Mr. Bhoopathi V, with a great passion to make tooling solutions for the farmers in Agri Industry. With a small Sales and Service Unit, the company grew rapidly to become One of the largest Distributors of Agricultural Equipments in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

The company soon expanded its Leading Agricultural range of products and tools that include Sprayers, Chain Saws, Brushcutters, Grass Trimmers, Pruners, Blowers, Garden Tools, Lawn Movers & Many More Ancillaries (Refer Products Page).

Decades

In Four decades, Bhuvana Enterprises earned a reputation for its products and services. The Business Tie ups with Industry Top Brands such as FALCON GARDEN TOOLS, ASPEE, STIHL & KISANKRAFT etc., are proven success.

Team of Experts are more focused towards sustaining the growth the company has made so far by managing the widened product portfolio, which comprises of widely demanded Agri, Horti and Farm Equipments and Tools. Bhuvana Enterprises proven success is based on the pivot of latest technology which provides a comprehensive list of benefits to our line of products. Moreover, we are availing the stature of leadership by rendering most efficient services that are trusted widely for our utmost professionalism, timely execution and strict quality controls.

Strong Commitments

Bhuvana Enterprises exhibits a strong commitment to attaining the highest levels of client satisfaction. Further, we believe in improvisation and bringing forth new and improved product designs to obtain utmost user safety and productivity to become One of the largest Distributors of Agricultural Equipments in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

Founder

Mr. Bhoopathi V, Chairman, has 48 years of experience in Agriculture industry. Graduate from a reputed institution in Tamilnadu, Began his career as a Marketing Executive in a Farm Equipment Manufacturing company. He took every opportunity to learn and execute. He expanded the sales market steadily in South India.With great ebility, In 1978, he founded Bhuvana Enterprises, a small Sales and Service unit of Agri and Farm Equipments for farmers in Coimbatore. He provided good product ranges to the farmers in local market. His humble nature earned him well connect in the Industry. He concentrated every single requirements of the farming people. He wanted every customer to be happy with the product and services which he offered. He found the demands / requirements of technologically updated Farm equipments. He understood the technology backlogs which carried him to source many product ranges from Various states like Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Punjab, Delhi etc. Ahead, He started import of materials from German, Japan and many other countries. With continuous progress, he widened his sales and service network to all parts of Tamilnadu.

Today, Mr.Bhoopathi delivers Diversified Product ranges for the people in Tamilnadu, Kerela and Karnataka. He makes every customer happy by delivering simple solutions for their farming needs with best technology. He says “Years don’t define your business, Customer satisfaction defines it”.
